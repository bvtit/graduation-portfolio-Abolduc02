Project Description

The OSHA 10 Hour General Industry certification is associated with a course that provided me with information and relevant saftey material to help me stay safe on the job. I completed this course in the classroom at my high school with one of my instructors who was certified to teach the 10 Hour General Industry course and award certificates of completion to those students who took the course. I have learned many different saftey skills in training for my OSHA certification, such as being able to recognize different kinds of hazards and dangers that could occur in the work place, being able to identify ways to protect myself from slipping, tripping, and falling, being able to handle and use ladders correctly, and many other safety skills. The certification proves that I know how to stay safe in the work place and that I know my safety rights and am protected by the Occupational Health and Saftey Administration.
