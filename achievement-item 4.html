Project Description

The CCNA Routing and Switching: Introduction to Networks course was the first course out a series of 4 that is provided by Cisco. In the Introduction to Networks course, I learned how to explain network technologies and how devices access local and remote network resources, describe router hardware, explain how switching operates in a small to medium-sized businiess network, design an IP addressing scheme to provide network connectivity for a small to medium-sized business network, configure intitial settings on a network device, implement basic network connectivity between devices, and configure monitoring tools available for small to medium-sized business networks. In completion of the course, I received a letter from the CEO of Cisco, Chuck Robbins, congratulating me on my high achievement on the Cisco course. I also receieved a certifcate that proves I have completed the course and was marked as a passing student by my instructor.
